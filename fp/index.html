mexican flowers through time

from dia de los muertos to diego rivera's “the flower seller,” mexican culture has been punctuated by a constant presence of flowers. journey through the months of the year to explore the intersection of flowers, arts, and culture in mexico.

about this project

this project is an exploration of the ways in which historical, artistic, and culinary elements of culture are influenced by local flowers in mexico.

how does our environment shape us?

how much do we learn about a culture when we learn about the nature in the region?

what are the implications when the cyclical timelines of nature intermix with the linear timelines of human history?
